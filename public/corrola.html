Corolla E120 is basically 9th gen cars under Corolla nameplate made by Toyota. Also has a hatchback variant called Allex. 9th gen corolla's are front engine which offers both front wheel and four wheel drive layout. This particular unit has a 1.5L 1NZ-FE heart with a 4 speed automatic transmission. This was one of Toyota's most versatile and most popular models ever produced. 9th gen Corolla's discontinued in 2008.
