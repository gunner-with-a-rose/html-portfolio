★Beetle AKA Slug Bug★

The Volkswagen Beetle was first introduced in 1938.But it came to production line in 1945 in Wolfsburg.1st model's engine had a displacement of 1131cc.In 1954,the engine displacement was increased to 1192cc for the first time,that managed to rise the power up from 30hp to 36 hp.The next year,after a decade from it's starting VW made it's one millionth Beetle in Wolfsburg.The same year,VW America was formed.In the 60's VW introduced the Automatic stick shift semi-automatic transmission option,essentially a regular beetle four speed manual with 1st gear removed,a vaccum operated clutch that disengaged anytime the shifter is touched.Finally,the model 1 died in 2003.Over it's incredible lifespan the model 1 Slug Bug sold more than 21,000,000 units.
